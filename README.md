## What this is
A structured credit analysis model for Bajaj Finance Ltd designed to assess business and financial risk and derive an implied credit rating outcome using an NBFC-focused framework.

## What it includes
- Company overview and financial statement capture
- Multi-year ratio and trend analysis
- NBFC-specific metrics (CAR, GNPA/NNPA, AUM growth, provision coverage)
- Industry benchmarking
- Risk scoring framework:
  - Business Risk (40%)
  - Financial Risk (60%)
- Credit rating mapping (AAA to D scale)
- Sensitivity and stress testing
- Executive summary output

## How to use
1. Start with the Executive Summary and Dashboard.
2. Review Business Risk and Financial Risk scoring sheets.
3. Adjust assumptions where enabled to observe rating sensitivity.
